Privacy Policy – SmartPDF

Last updated: July 2026  ·  Bundle ID: com.hifivetech.smartpdf

SmartPDF ("the App") is a free, open-source document scanner and PDF reader developed by HiFive Tech. This policy explains what data the App accesses, how it is used, and your rights as a user.

1. Data We Do Not Collect

The App does not collect, transmit, store remotely, or share any personal data. There are no analytics SDKs, advertising SDKs, crash-reporting services, or remote servers involved. Your documents, images, and metadata never leave your device.

2. Data Stored Locally on Your Device

All data the App creates stays entirely on your device:

  • PDF files – saved to the app's private documents directory (AppDocuments/smart_pdf/files/).
  • Thumbnail images – low-resolution previews stored in AppDocuments/smart_pdf/thumbs/.
  • Document metadata – title, page count, timestamps, and favourite status stored in a local SQLite database. No cloud sync.

You can delete all stored data at any time by removing documents within the App or by uninstalling the App.

3. Device Permissions

The App requests only the permissions required for its core features:

  • Camera – to capture document pages for scanning.
  • Photo Library / Media – to import images you choose. The App reads only files you explicitly select; it does not browse or index your gallery.
  • Storage (Android) – to save and read PDF files on supported Android versions.

Permissions are requested at the time they are needed and can be revoked at any time through your device settings. Revoking a permission disables the corresponding feature but does not affect data already saved.

4. Sharing

The App includes a Share function that lets you send PDF files to other apps (e.g. email, messaging, cloud storage) using your operating system's native share sheet. Any sharing is initiated solely by you; the App does not automatically send files anywhere.

5. Third-Party Libraries

The App uses open-source Flutter packages for PDF creation, viewing, image processing, and file sharing. These libraries run entirely on-device and do not transmit data to third parties. A full list of dependencies and their licences is available in the App under Menu → Open Source Licenses and in the source repository.

6. Children's Privacy

The App does not collect any personal information from anyone, including children under 13. It is safe to use in any age group.

7. Changes to This Policy

If this policy is updated, the revised version will be published at this URL with an updated date. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
